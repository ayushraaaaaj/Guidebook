The Problem We're Solving

Many junior students lack proper guidance and mentorship, struggling to navigate academic challenges, career decisions, and campus life effectively.

Our Solution

Guidebook bridges this gap by collecting experiences from senior students and transforming them into accessible, AI-powered guidance that's easy to understand and implement.

AI-Powered Text/Speech Transformation

Our core technology converts senior student experiences into:

  • Structured Guidance: Organized, easy-to-follow advice
  • Key Point Extraction: Important insights highlighted
  • Speech Synthesis: Audio guidance for better accessibility
  • Simple Language: Complex experiences made understandable
